# Operational Worlds Development Roadmap

## Guiding Principle

Operational Worlds is developed through **parallel evolution**.

The framework is not constructed by completing theory before methodology, or methodology before engineering. Instead, each domain evolves alongside the others.

Insights generated in one domain continuously inform and refine the rest.

Theory shapes engineering.

Engineering reveals limitations in theory.

Methodology guides research.

Research generates new theory.

This recursive process is considered a defining characteristic of the Operational Worlds framework.

---

# The Parallel Evolution Principle


The theory, methodology, research, and engineering of Operational Worlds are developed concurrently. Progress within any domain may necessitate refinement of the others. The framework evolves through continuous dialogue between conceptual understanding, practical experimentation, and computational implementation.

No domain is considered permanently complete.

Every domain remains open to refinement through evidence.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is not simply to produce software or artworks.

The objective is to establish Operational Worlds as a coherent research discipline in which theory, methodology, engineering, and artistic practice continuously evolve together.

Every Operational World contributes to this evolution.

Every experiment refines the framework.

Every refinement enables new forms of research.

The framework therefore remains a living system rather than a finished product.
